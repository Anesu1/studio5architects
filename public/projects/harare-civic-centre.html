Civic & Institutional

Harare Civic
Centre Expansion.

A civic architecture that prioritizes transparency and accessibility, creating a new interface between the government and the people of Harare.

The expansion of the Harare Civic Centre was conceived as an exercise in "porosity". We sought to break down the monolithic nature of typical institutional buildings, introducing open courtyards, shaded walkways, and a public atrium that invites the city in. The design responds to the local climate through high-performance glazing and a secondary veil of perforated screen-work that manages solar gain while allowing natural ventilation.
