Who We Are

A Practice
Built on
Conviction.

Founded in Harare in 2014, Studio5 Architects has grown into one of Zimbabwe's most respected architectural practices. Our team of registered architects, graduate designers, and technical specialists works across the full spectrum of building design — from carefully crafted private residences to large-scale civic, commercial, and institutional infrastructure.

We believe that good architecture is not a luxury — it is a fundamental act of care. A well-designed building improves the daily lives of the people who use it, contributes positively to its street and neighbourhood, and maintains its quality over time. These are not abstract ideals; they are the practical standards we hold ourselves to on every commission.

Our process is transparent and genuinely collaborative. Clients are partners — not just approvers. We share our thinking early, respond to feedback openly, and remain engaged long after construction is complete. That continuing relationship is something we value as much as the building itself.

What Guides Us

Our Design
Principles

Every project we undertake is shaped by a set of core principles that define how we think, work, and deliver. These are not abstract ideals — they are the practical commitments we make to every client, every community, and every building we put our name to.

Design Excellence

Architecture is in the details. Every material, joint, threshold, and proportion is interrogated until it is exactly right — because the quality of a building is ultimately the sum of its smallest decisions.

Client Collaboration

A great brief produces a great building. We invest time in understanding not just what clients want to build, but why — and that understanding informs every design decision from concept through to construction.

Environmental Responsibility

Zimbabwe's climate is an asset, not an obstacle. We use orientation, natural ventilation, shading, and thermal mass as primary design tools — reducing energy dependence while creating more comfortable, enduring spaces.

Social Impact

We are active participants in Zimbabwe's development — not observers. Through pro-bono design work, university scholarships, and mentorship of emerging architects, we help build the profession as well as the buildings.
